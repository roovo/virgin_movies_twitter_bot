A group of actors shooting an action movie end up wrecking the project due to their massive egos. Ben Stiller is an action hero trying to change his image. Jack Black is a drug-addicted actor and Robert Downey Jr. has dyed his skin to play a black soldier.

Film Trivia

  • Jack Black's character is based on Chris Farley, a comic actor who died from a drug overdose.
  • Ben Stiller had been developing ideas for this movie 10 years before it was even made. He decided on this plot after seeing how ridiculous it was that many other actors attended fake boot camps before making war films.
  • Robert Downey Jr.'s character was originally written as Irish but Downey Jr. said it would be easier for him to improvise lines in an Australian accent.
  • A number of actors appear as themselves in this movie including Jon Voight, Jennifer Love Hewitt, Jason Bateman and Alicia Silverstone.
  • Justin Theroux, the producer and co-writer of this movie, is the cousin of renowned documentary filmmaker Louis Theroux.
