Film Trivia

  • During a French TV Show the actress and singer Marie Laforet announced she would be appearing in this film. She eventually took no part in it.
  • The actor who plays Jean, Gad Elmaleh, is a popular stand-up comedian in France.
  • Pierre Salvadori both wrote and directed this film.
  • Audrey Tautou has said that she does not think her character in this film is a bad person. She says that all the men in the film should know what sort of person they are dealing with from the outset.
